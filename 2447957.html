Development Journal

Week 1 - Setup & Visual Style:
Installed Unity via Unity Hub and configured the project. Defined the visual style — realistic and gritty — fitting our game's tone. Planned distinct looks for characters and enemies.

Week 2 - Level 01 Planning:
Planned Level 01 as a gladiator-style arena. Researched arena concepts and started layout design with ramps and obstacles.

Week 3 - Level Design & Asset Creation:
Created Level 01 base using Unreal, to be exported to Unity. Began modeling assets like the stadium, pillars, and lighting rigs.

Week 4 - Blockout:
Created the basic level structure using placeholder geometry to visualize scale and movement pathways.

Week 5 - Asset Placement:
Selected and positioned environment assets, ensuring alignment and thematic consistency.

Week 6 - Lighting Setup:
Created a spotlight-focused mood using warm tones and directional lights to enhance visibility and tone.

Week 7 - Sound Design:
Planned immersive soundscape including crowd noise, clashing metal, and spatial effects for engine sounds.

Week 8 - Gameplay Testing:
Conducted basic movement and combat tests. Adjusted ramps and sightlines based on player flow analysis.

Week 9 - Visual Polish:
Refined materials, added dirt/wear effects, and used decals to add realism and depth to the arena.

Week 10 - UI/UX:
Sketched HUD design focused on clarity: health, ammo/boost bars, possibly a minimap for larger levels.

Week 11 - Internal Playtest:
Feedback showed cramped areas. Reworked arena layout for better car combat spacing and obstacle flow.

Week 12 - Difficulty Progression:
Designed level changes including enemy spawn pacing and arena hazards to increase challenge over time.

Week 13 - Final Polish:
Refined visuals, adjusted lighting/fog for clarity, and conducted design retrospective to evaluate project goals.
